Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SIUU Studio collects, uses, and discloses information about you in compliance with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA).

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information when you use our platform:

  • Identifiers: Name, alias, IP address, and email address (if provided during account creation).
  • Commercial Information: Projects created, tools utilized, and user engagement metrics.
  • Internet or Network Activity: Browsing history, search history within the app, and interactions with our AI models.
  • Sensitive Personal Information: We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information unless explicitly provided by you in your video prompts or text inputs.

2. Usage and Sharing of Information

We use your data primarily to provide, personalize, and improve our AI creation tools. SIUU Studio does not sell your personal information to third parties. We only share information with authorized service providers necessary to operate the platform, under strict confidentiality agreements.

3. Your California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

Under California law, consumers have specific rights regarding their personal information:

  • Right to Know: You may request details about the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you over the past 12 months.
  • Right to Delete: You may request the deletion of personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Right to Opt-Out: While we do not sell your data, you have the right to opt-out of the sharing of your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights. You will not be denied goods or services or charged different prices.

4. How to Submit a Request

To exercise your Right to Know, Delete, or Opt-Out, please submit a verifiable consumer request by emailing us at privacy@siuustudio.com.

5. Accessibility

This Privacy Policy is designed to be easily readable and accessible. We review and update this policy at least annually. If you are a user with a disability and require this policy in an alternative format, please contact us at support@siuustudio.com.

6. AI Technology & Transparency (AB 2013 / SB 942)

SIUU Studio's AI features are powered by the Cerebras AI API. We have not fine-tuned, retrained, or substantially modified the underlying base model. All AI behavior is guided by proprietary system instructions designed to optimize content strategy for creators. SIUU Studio is the deploying provider of this AI tool, not the developer of the underlying model.

SIUU Studio does not collect neural data, biometric data, or any data generated by the brain or nervous system, as defined under California's 2026 CCPA updates. All AI-generated content produced by our tools is intended as creative suggestions only. Users remain fully responsible for reviewing, editing, and verifying any AI output before publishing or distributing it.
