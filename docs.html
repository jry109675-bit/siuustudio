Overview

What is SIUU Studio?

SIUU Studio is an all-in-one AI workspace built for content creators. Instead of juggling a dozen different apps to plan, write, and optimize your videos, SIUU Studio puts every tool you need in one place - all connected to your projects.

Every tool is powered by AI, which means you get real output - not templates. The AI adapts to your niche, your audience, and your content style. And your AI Coach is always available to give you specific, tailored advice based on whatever you're working on right now.

Getting Started

Quick Start

You can be generating your first video idea in under two minutes. Here's how:

01

Create a free account

Head to siuustudio.com/siuu-auth.html and sign up with your email. No credit card needed - the free plan gives you full access to all tools and the AI Coach.

02

Open a tool and describe your niche

Click any tool from the left sidebar - for example, Idea Generator. Type in your channel niche (e.g. "productivity for college students" or "budget fitness"), choose your platform, and hit Generate.

03

Save your output to a project

Every AI output has a Save to Project button. This keeps your ideas, hooks, scripts, and shot plans organized and makes them available to the AI Coach.

04

Ask your AI Coach anything

Tap the orange chat button in the bottom-right corner at any time. Your Coach already knows what's in your active project and can give you specific advice for your exact video.

🚀

Pro tip: Start with the Idea Generator, pick your favorite idea, then run Hook Analyzer on your opening line before you ever pick up a camera. This alone will dramatically improve your retention.

Core Feature

Projects Studio

Projects Studio is your creative filing cabinet. Every video idea you work on gets its own project folder that stores all the AI outputs you've generated for it: the idea, the hook score, the script, the shot plan, and more.

Creating a project

In the Projects Studio tab (accessible from the left sidebar), type a name in the "New project name" field and click Create. You can also let the AI tools auto-create a project the first time you save an output - you'll see a prompt in the top bar when this happens.

Switching projects

Click any project card in the grid to make it the active project. The top bar will show the project name and all your AI tools will now save to and read from that project.

Searching projects

Use the search bar at the top of the Projects Studio grid to filter by name. The search is instant and case-insensitive. If no results match, a "No projects found" message appears. The search bar only appears when you have at least one project.

Exporting a project

Click the download icon on any project card to export all of its saved content as a file. This is useful for sharing your plan with an editor or keeping an offline backup.

Deleting a project

Click the trash icon on a project card. You'll be asked to confirm before anything is deleted. Deleted projects are removed from cloud sync and cannot be recovered, so make sure to export first if you need a copy.

☁️

Projects sync automatically. Everything saves to your account in real time. Switch between devices and your projects will always be up to date.

AI Tool

Idea Generator

The Idea Generator produces scroll-stopping video concepts tailored to your niche. It analyzes trending formats, evergreen angles, and viral patterns to give you ideas that are actually worth filming.

How to use it

Enter your channel niche (be specific - "budget home gym workouts for beginners" will give better results than just "fitness"), choose your target platform (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram), then click Generate. You'll get multiple ideas with brief descriptions of the format and angle.

Getting better results

The more specific your niche description, the more targeted your ideas will be. Include your target audience if you know it. You can run it multiple times to get a fresh batch of ideas.

AI Tool

Hook Analyzer

The first 3 seconds of a video decide whether a viewer stays or scrolls. The Hook Analyzer scores your opening line and tells you exactly what's working and what isn't - before you ever hit record.

How to use it

Paste your planned opening line or on-screen text hook into the input field and click Analyze. You'll receive a score out of 10 along with a breakdown of why it's strong or weak, and specific suggestions to improve it.

What makes a good hook

Hooks that score highly typically do one of three things immediately: create curiosity ("You've been doing this wrong"), make a bold claim ("I doubled my views in 30 days"), or promise a specific outcome ("Watch this before you buy"). The Hook Analyzer will guide you toward whichever pattern fits your video best.

AI Tool

Title & Thumbnail Lab

Your title and thumbnail are the only two things a viewer sees before deciding whether to click. The Title & Thumbnail Lab generates combinations engineered for maximum click-through rate based on what's proven to work in your niche.

How to use it

Describe your video concept and the Lab will generate multiple title options with thumbnail concept descriptions. Each title is analyzed for CTR potential, emotional hooks, and SEO-friendliness.

Thumbnail concepts

The Lab generates descriptive thumbnail concepts - not images. These are production briefs you can hand to a designer or use as a reference when making your own. They describe the layout, text, expression, and color palette that will perform best for your specific topic.

AI Tool

Shot Planner

Walking into a shoot without a shot list wastes time and leads to boring footage. The Shot Planner gives you a complete scene-by-scene breakdown of everything you need to capture before you pick up a camera.

How to use it

Describe your video idea and the Shot Planner will produce a structured list of shots: your main talking-head moments, B-roll suggestions, close-ups to grab, and pacing notes. You can also supply a script and it will generate a shot list directly from it.

AI Tool

Script Writer

The Script Writer generates full, ready-to-film video scripts - not just outlines. Every script includes a hook, intro, structured body sections, and a call to action, all tuned to the platform and format you choose.

How to use it

Give the Script Writer your video idea, your niche, your target platform, and an optional tone (educational, entertaining, motivational, etc.). It will write a complete script you can start filming from immediately.

Editing your script

All scripts are editable directly in the app. Treat the AI output as a first draft - adjust phrasing, add personal stories, and make it sound like you. The AI gives you the structure; you bring the personality.

AI Tool

Editing Guide

Once you've filmed, the Editing Guide gives you a custom post-production roadmap: where to cut, what music fits the mood, when to add text overlays, and how to structure the edit for maximum retention.

How to use it

Describe the video you've filmed (or paste your script) and the Editing Guide will produce a timestamped editing plan with specific recommendations for pacing, transitions, and engagement tactics tailored to your platform.

AI Tool

Caption Generator

A strong caption increases watch time, saves, and shares. The Caption Generator produces 3 distinct caption options for every video, each optimized for different goals: reach, engagement, or saves.

What you get

Each caption option comes with platform-optimized hashtags, a recommended posting time for your niche, and power-word callouts explaining why the caption is structured the way it is.

AI Tool - Live Web

Trend Analyzer

The Trend Analyzer pulls live web data to show you what's actually trending in your niche right now - not what was trending six months ago when an AI model was last trained.

🌐

This tool uses live web search. It queries multiple sources in real time before generating your trend report. Results reflect what's happening on social platforms today.

How to use it

Enter your niche and click Analyze. The tool will search the web, identify the top 5 trending topics and formats right now, and give you specific video angles to pursue and a note on what's already oversaturated to avoid.

AI Tool

Content Calendar

Consistency is the single biggest factor in channel growth. The Content Calendar builds you a full 4-week posting schedule with a content idea, format suggestion, and goal for each video - so you always know what to make next.

How to use it

Tell the tool your niche and how often you can post per week. It will generate a realistic, niche-specific content plan that balances trending formats with evergreen content for long-term growth.

AI Coach

What is AI Coach?

The AI Coach is your personal content strategist, available 24/7 inside the app. Unlike a generic chatbot, the Coach reads your currently active project - your idea, hook score, script, shot plan - and gives advice that's specific to your exact video, not boilerplate tips you've already read a hundred times.

How to open it

Tap the orange chat bubble in the bottom-right corner of the app at any time. It's available on every screen, so you can ask a question mid-script or mid-edit without losing your place.

What it knows

When you have an active project selected, the Coach automatically loads the context of everything in that project. You don't need to paste in your script or describe your idea - it already has it. Just ask your question and get an answer that actually fits your situation.

Chat history

Coaching sessions are saved per project. Close the app, come back tomorrow, and your conversation picks up exactly where it left off. The Coach remembers what you were working on.

With your hook score saved to the project, the Coach gives you specific rewrites - not generic advice about "creating curiosity".

Account

Sign In & Sign Up

SIUU Studio uses email-based authentication. You'll receive a magic link or OTP to verify your email - no passwords to remember.

Creating an account

Go to siuustudio.com/siuu-auth.html and enter your email. You'll be asked to agree to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, then receive a verification email. Click the link in that email and you're in.

Signing back in

Return to the sign-in page, enter your email, and a new login link will be sent to your inbox. Links expire after a short time for security, so use it promptly.

Password reset

If you set a password and need to reset it, use the "Forgot password" link on the sign-in page. A reset link will be emailed to you.
